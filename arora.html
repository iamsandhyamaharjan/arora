NICE AND COMFORTABLE PLACE TO STAY

Arora hotel is strategically placed on the top of Nagarkot hill, thus offering the best view of the spectacular show upfront. It encapsulates all the elements that set Nagarkot apart. It showcases nature at her best, and thereby catapults its dwellers to the romantic self and promises of a splendid stay.Enjoy a family holiday or quality moments with friends, this holiday get-away is perfect for both. The place is also ideal for hosting seminars or picnics. However, if your target is solitude, do not fret; the place molds itself according to your need.
